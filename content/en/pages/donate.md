## Donate via Wau Holland Stiftung

One of our sponsors, the [Wau Holland Stiftung](https://www.wauland.de/en/),
hosts a dedicated donation page for Katzenpost:

**[Donate to Katzenpost via WHS](https://www.wauland.de/en/donate/anonymity-networks/#21)**

Donations through WHS are tax-deductible in Germany. The WHS page also describes how to donate in a variety of cryptocurrencies.

## Donate via GitHub Sponsors

The project also accepts recurring or one-time sponsorship through GitHub Sponsors:

**[Sponsor Katzenpost on GitHub](https://github.com/sponsors/katzenpost)**

Donations through GitHub Sponsors are not tax-deductible.

## What donations fund

- Software development
- Protocol research
- Project infrastructure and mixnet operations
